Training Data Overview

To train the web application LadderWeb, we used annotated subcorpora from DisDir (Nuzzo & Cortés Velásquez, 2020; Brocca et al. 2023) and Ladder (Brocca & Nuzzo 2024).
The data has been pragmatically annotated (see tag Annotation criteria).

The application has been trained on 864 messages in Italian (L1+L2; requests and cancellations) and 100 messages in German (L1; cancellations).

The data used for the training of LadderWeb is a selection from a larger dataset. To consult the entire dataset, please consult the corpus Ladder and DisDir in the following online archive:
Brocca, N. (2024). Ladder: a corpus for pragmatic competences in Italian L1/L2 [Data set]. https://hdl.handle.net/21.11115/0000-0011-83CC-3
Nuzzo, E., & Cortés Velásquez, D. (2024). DisDir: Cancellations and Other Refusal Strategies (N. Brocca, Ed.) [Data set]. https://hdl.handle.net/21.11115/0000-0011-83CD-2

Further information about the corpus and how it was elicited is available under the following link: DOI 10.5281/zenodo.4895218.
