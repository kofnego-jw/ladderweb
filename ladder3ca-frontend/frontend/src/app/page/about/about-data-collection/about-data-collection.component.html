Data Collection

Data were collected from April 2020 to April 2021 using a Discourse Completion Task (DCT). The dataset comprises emails and instant messages. The participants include (i) German learners of Italian, ranging from levels A2 to C1 according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), most of whom are students residing in Tyrol, Austria, and (ii) native Italian speakers, primarily students from Rome, Italy. Data from the learners were gathered by students enrolled in the undergraduate seminar "Insegnare la pragmatica," which is included in the compulsory module 2b for student teachers at the Institute of Didactics, University of Innsbruck. Data from the native speakers were predominantly collected from students studying foreign languages at the University of Roma Tre.

The DCTs were administered via online questionnaires. In addition to the texts, metadata providing sociolinguistic information about the participants (age, self-assessed language level, place of residence, native language, etc.) were collected through an online questionnaire. The DCTs were designed to elicit linguistic acts of request and refusal across varying levels of social distance and through different media (Taguchi & Roever, 2017, pp. 85, 231; Hinger et al., 2018, p. 148). These tasks aimed to prompt various speech acts (requests and refusals) with varying degrees of formality (in study/work or leisure contexts), directed at different individuals (lecturer, friend, boss), and executed via different communication channels (email or instant messaging). The scenarios were crafted to reflect authentic situations encountered by the students.

The examined situations are described below:

Email Communication Scenarios:

  • High Social Distance
    • Scenario 1: The sender requests a favor or concession to which they are not inherently entitled.
    • Scenario 2: The sender makes a request for something they are entitled to.

WhatsApp Message Scenarios:

  • Low Social Distance:
    • Scenario 1: A request is made.
    • Scenario 2: A request is rejected.
    • Scenario 3: An invitation is cancelled on short notice. (a).
    • Scenario 4: An invitation is cancelled on short notice (b).
  • Medium Social Distance:
    • Scenario 5: A request is made.
    • Scenario 6: A request is rejected.
    • Scenario 7: An invitation is cancelled on short notice. (a).
    • Scenario 8: An invitation is cancelled on short notice (b).
    • Scenario 9: An invitation is cancelled on short notice (c).

The WhatsApp messages, exemplifying instant messaging text types, were produced directly on mobile phones. The accompanying metadata were later associated with the respective messages in an Excel spreadsheet, ensuring all personal data were anonymized.

Prompts in Italian:

Email Scenarios:

  1. Mail: Scenario Description in Italian: Immagina di star facendo un corso con il Dr. Nicola Brocca. Domani devi fare una presentazione in classe. Non hai avuto tempo per studiare perché dovevi prepararti a un esame di inglese e ti accorgi che il materiale da presentare è più di quello che avevi previsto. Scrivi una mail al professore: la tua speranza è spostare la presentazione.

    English Translation: Imagine you are taking a course with Dr. Nicola Brocca. Tomorrow you have to give a presentation in class. You had no time to study because you had to prepare for an English exam, and you realize that there is more material to present than you had imagined. You write an email to the professor: Your hope is to reschedule the presentation.

  2. Mail: Scenario Description in Italian: Hai fatto un corso con il Dr. Brocca. Hai consegnato il tuo portfolio il 01.02.2020 adesso è il 01.03.2020 e non hai ancora ricevuto il voto. Ti serve il voto per registrarti per una borsa di studio. Manda una mail al prof.: il tuo obiettivo è ricevere il voto al più presto.

    English Translation: You have taken a course with Dr. Brocca. You turned in your portfolio on 02/01/2020, it is now 03/01/2020 and you have not received the grade yet. You need the grade to register for a scholarship. Send an email to the professor: Your goal is to receive the grade as soon as possible.

WhatsApp Scenarios:

  1. Request Library Card: Scenario Description in Italian: Sei in Erasmus in Italia. Hai creato una chat con 10 compagni di corso. Hai perso la tua tessera della biblioteca e hai bisogno di un libro entro domani. Chiedi se qualcuno può aiutarti, per esempio, prestandoti la sua tessera. Cosa scrivi?

    English Translation: You are participating in the Erasmus program in Italy. You've created a chat group with 10 classmates. You lost your library card and need a book by tomorrow. You want to ask if someone can help you, for example, by lending you their card. What do you write?

  2. Declining a Friend’s Request for Help: Scenario Description in Italian: Ricevi un messaggio da un amico che partecipa a un seminario con te: "Ciao, sono a corto di tempo. Ho visto che hai preso 30 all'esame. Potresti aiutarmi e restare con me in biblioteca oggi?" Non vuoi aiutare il tuo amico. Come reagisci?

    English Translation: You receive a message from a friend attending a seminar with you: "Hello, I'm running out of time. I saw you got a 30 on the exam. Could you help me and stay with me in the library today?" You don't want to help your friend. How do you respond?

  3. Cancelling Plans to Go to the Cinema with a Friend: Scenario Description in Italian: Cinque giorni fa hai promesso a un amico che stasera andreste al cinema insieme. Però hai cambiato idea. Cosa fai? Cosa scrivi?

    English Translation: Five days ago, you promised a friend that you would go to the movies together tonight. But you've changed your mind. What do you do? What do you write?

  4. Cancelling a Dinner Invitation: Scenario Description in Italian: Sei stato/a invitato/a a cena da un/a amico/a per stasera e hai accettato, ma all’ultimo momento mandi un messaggio per dire che non vai. Che cosa scrivi?

    English Translation: You have been invited to dinner tonight by a friend and have accepted, but at the last minute you send a message to say that you are not going. What do you write?

  5. Request Parking Badge: Scenario Description in Italian:
    Sei al lavoro e hai smarrito il documento elettronico per entrare nel parcheggio. Sei nuovo in questo gruppo di lavoro e hai solo il numero del tuo diretto superiore. Gli mandi un messaggio per chiedergli se ti può aiutare.

    English Translation: You are at work and have lost your electronic badge to enter the parking lot. You are new to this work group and only have the number of your direct supervisor. You send him/her a message and ask if he/she can help you.

  6. Declining a Supervisor’s Request to Stay Late: Scenario Description in Italian: Ricevi un messaggio dal tuo superiore: "Gentile collega, domani abbiamo una scadenza importante. Puoi restare in ufficio oltre l'orario di lavoro oggi?" Non vuoi restare oltre l'orario. Come reagisci?

    English Translation: You receive a message from your supervisor: "Dear colleague, tomorrow we have an important deadline. Can you stay in the office after hours today?" You don't want to stay late. How do you respond?

  7. Cancelling a Business Dinner Appointment: Scenario Description in Italian: Cinque giorni fa hai promesso al tuo superiore che oggi saresti andato a una cena di lavoro. Però devi disdire. Cosa fai?

    English Translation: Five days ago, you promised your superior that you would attend a business dinner today. However, you need to cancel. What do you do?

  8. Cancelling a Party Invitation: Scenario Description in Italian: Un/a conoscente ti ha invitato/a a una festicciola a casa sua per stasera, e hai accettato, ma all’ultimo momento mandi un messaggio per dire che non vai. Che cosa scrivi?

    English Translation: An acquaintance has invited you to a little party at his or her house tonight, and you have accepted, but at the last moment you send a message saying that you are not going. What do you write?

  9. Cancelling an Invitation to Have Drinks Together: Scenario Description in Italian: Il/la tuo/a nuovo/a vicino/a di casa, con cui hai recentemente avuto una discussione per una questione di parcheggio, ti ha invitato/a a un aperitivo tra amici sulla sua terrazza per venerdì sera. Tu hai accettato, ma all’ultimo momento mandi un messaggio per dire che non vai. Che cosa scrivi?

    English Translation: Your new neighbour, with whom you recently had an argument about the parking situation, has invited you to an aperitif with friends on his terrace on Friday evening. You have accepted, but at the last moment you send a message saying that you are not going. What do you write?

Data Processing and Archiving: The corpus, initially collected in .xlsx format, was exported to XML and CSV formats. It was ensured that emoticons and special characters were preserved unchanged during the conversion process. These formats facilitate long-term archiving and significantly ease data exchange.
